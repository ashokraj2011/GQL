Hackathon Core Teams & Roles

1. Ideas Generation Team

Sub‑Role Recommended Profile Key Deliverables When It’s “Done”
Regional Track Lead Senior IC or EM familiar with local pain‑points Curated list of top 5 problems per track; 1‑pager per problem (impact, KPIs, data access) Backlog signed‑off by Business Sponsor
Facilitator / Design‑Thinking Coach User‑researcher or Agile coach Ideation workshop agendas; Miro / Jamboard templates ≥2 workshops delivered, ≥80 % positive feedback
Data Scout Analytics / BI engineer Confirm data availability & access paths for each idea All shortlisted ideas have data‑readiness score
Business Liaison Product / Domain manager Executive summaries to business VPs; secure co‑sponsors ≥1 sponsor per track
Innovation Evangelist Enthusiastic senior dev Pre‑hack “Inspiration Talks”; Q&A office hours ≥2 talks + office hour per region

2. Organising Team

Sub‑Role What They Handle Critical Outputs
Program Manager (Lead) Budget, master plan, exec reporting Master Gantt & risk register
Logistics Coordinator Venue, catering, swag, travel Event playbook & vendor contracts
Platform & DevOps Lead Cloud credits, Git, CI/CD, SSO On‑demand hack infra & “Hello‑World” repo
Finance / Legal Liaison PO creation, NDA / IP agreements, prize compliance Signed T&Cs & PO numbers
Volunteer Manager Recruit & brief helpers; shift rota 24 / 7 support roster during hack days

3. Branding & Communication Team

Sub‑Role Artefacts Success Metric
Creative Director (Lead) Logo, style‑guide, slide master Visual kit approved within 2 weeks
Content Strategist Newsletter copy, intranet blog, executive comms 70 %+ email open‑rate
Social Media Manager LinkedIn / Twitter posts, short videos 2× growth in hashtag reach
Multimedia Producer Teaser trailer, highlight reel, live‑stream overlays Final video published ≤1 week post‑event

4. Evaluation Team

Sub‑Role Responsibilities Key Assets
Head Judge (Lead) Final arbitration, rubric owner Signed‑off rubric with weightings
Tech SME Panel ×3 Code quality, scalability, originality Scorecards per project
Business SME Panel ×3 ROI, market fit, feasibility Business viability scores
D&I Advocate Check bias, inclusive language, accessibility Bias report & mitigation checklist
Scorekeeper / Auditor Collect ballots, crunch numbers, audit trail Leaderboard & signed results file

5. Prizes / Awards / Tokens Team

Sub‑Role Example Tasks KPI
Rewards Lead Define prize tiers (₹50k, gadgets, certifications) Prizes approved & sourced
Sponsor Outreach Approach vendors / partners for goodies or credits ₹ x lakhs in sponsorship value
Gamification Designer Achievement badges, micro‑rewards, quizzes ≥80 % participants earn ≥1 token
Swag & Shipping Coordinator T‑shirts, stickers, global postage tracking 100 % swag delivered pre‑event
